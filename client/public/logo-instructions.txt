TO ADD YOUR LOGO TO RECEIPTS:

1. Convert your "logo zamam.pdf" to PNG or JPG format
   - You can use: https://www.pdf2png.com/ or https://cloudconvert.com/pdf-to-png
   
2. Save the converted image as: logo.png
   
3. Place it in this folder: D:\Next\InvoiceForge\client\public\

4. The logo will automatically appear on all receipts!

Recommended logo size: 500x500 pixels (square) or 800x200 pixels (wide)
Supported formats: PNG (with transparency), JPG
